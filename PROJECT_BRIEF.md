# PROJECT BRIEF — BOSPHORAS

> IMPORTANT PROJECT MEMORY — READ BEFORE BUILDING ANYTHING

We are building a premium multilingual website for a brand called **BOSPHORAS**.

Domain: **bosphoras.com**

Bosphoras is **not** a real estate agency.
Bosphoras is **not** a travel agency.
Bosphoras is **not** a generic concierge.

Bosphoras is a **private desk / private office in Turkey** for international investors, entrepreneurs, families, private clients and high-net-worth individuals who want one trusted contact for everything in Turkey.

## Core promise
"One trusted desk for everything Turkey."

French positioning: "Votre bureau privé en Turquie."

## Legal boundaries (CRITICAL)

Bosphoras coordinates access to selected professionals.
Bosphoras does **not** replace lawyers, doctors, tax advisors, accountants, insurers or regulated travel agencies.
Legal, tax, medical and insurance decisions must be confirmed directly with licensed professionals.

- Do not promise guaranteed residence.
- Do not promise guaranteed tax results.
- Do not promise guaranteed investment returns.
- Do not promise medical outcomes.
- Do not claim official partnerships unless `partnerStatus = official`.
- Do not use logos of Amex, Visa, Mastercard, Revolut, chambers of commerce or hotels without permission.

## Kavlak Law / legal framework
The project has access to a reputed Turkish legal partner: **Fırat Kavlak / Kavlak Law**.

Use careful wording: *"Legal and tax matters can be coordinated with independent Turkish legal professionals, including Kavlak Law Firm, where appropriate."*

Do not imply Bosphoras itself gives legal advice. Do not imply all legal work is automatically done by Kavlak. Do not imply official guarantee.
